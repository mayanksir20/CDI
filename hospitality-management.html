Hospitality & Tourism Industry

CDI – Association of Hospitality and Tourism Industry is a professional body dedicated to strengthening the hospitality and tourism sector through collaboration, skill development, and industry support. CDI connects institutions, professionals, and tourism enterprises to promote quality standards, responsible practices, and sustainable growth across the industry.
